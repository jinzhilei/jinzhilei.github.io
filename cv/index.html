Highlighted Publications

  1. Lei, J., 2020. A general mathematical framework for understanding the behavior of heterogeneous stem cell regeneration. J Theor Biol, 492, 110196. Establishes a general mathematical framework for modeling heterogeneous stem cell regeneration dynamics, proposing the core concept of kinetotype in stem cell biology.

  2. Yu, Y., Situ, Q., Jia, W., Li, J., Wu, Q.*, Lei, J.*, 2019. Data driven mathematical modelling reveals the dynamic mechanism of MSC-induced neovascularization. FASEB J., 33, 75–81.

  3. Guo, Y., Nie, Q., MacLean, A., Li, Y., Lei, J.*, Li, S.*, 2017. Multiscale modeling of inflammation-induced tumorigenesis reveals competing oncogenic and oncoprotective roles for inflammation. Cancer Research, 77(22), 6429–6441. Develops a multiscale model for cancer development revealing multiple pathways from inflammation to tumorigenesis.

  4. Lei, J., 2016. On the classification of polynomial differential operators with respect to the type of first integrals. J. Diff. Equat., 260, 1991–2025. Presents a complete classification of second polynomial systems through differential algebra.

  5. Lei, J., Levin, S.*, Nie, Q.*, 2014. Mathematical model of adult stem cell regeneration with cross-talk between genetic and epigenetic regulation. Proc. Natl. Acad. Sci USA, 111, E880–E881. Connects epigenetic state transition with population dynamics in stem cell regeneration. Commented on PNAS, 111(2014), 3653–3654.

  6. Wang, Z., Li, X., Lei, J.*, 2014. Second moment boundedness of linear stochastic delay differential equations. Stochastic Processes and their Applications, 124, 586–612.

  7. Lei, J., Nonlinear differential Galois theory. arXiv:math.CA/0608492. Proposes a definition of the differential Galois group for second-order differential equations and proves the related Galois theory.
