On July 18, 2025, Prof. Jinzhi Lei was invited to give an academic lecture at the [School of Mathematical Sciences, Inner Mongolia University](https://math.imu.edu.cn/). The talk, titled **"Using Mathematics to Study Cancer (用数学研究癌症)"**, provided an accessible yet rigorous overview of mathematical approaches to cancer biology.

Topics covered included:
- Multi-scale modelling of tumour evolution
- The mathematics of drug resistance and relapse
- Optimal adaptive therapy design
- The Waddington landscape as a mathematical metaphor for cancer cell plasticity

The lecture attracted a wide audience from mathematics, biology, and medical informatics, reflecting the growing interdisciplinary interest in mathematical oncology.
