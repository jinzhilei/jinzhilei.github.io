On November 8, 2024, Prof. Jinzhi Lei delivered an invited in-person academic lecture at [Tianjin University's Center for Applied Mathematics](https://cam.tju.edu.cn/). The talk, titled **"Heterogeneity, Plasticity, Entropy, and Waddington Landscape in Cell Proliferation Dynamics"**, explored a mathematical framework for understanding the diversity and adaptability of cell types through the lens of Waddington's epigenetic landscape.

The talk introduced stochastic models capturing cell-type transitions, the role of epigenetic entropy in determining cell fate flexibility, and implications for understanding cancer cell plasticity and treatment resistance.

We thank the organizers at Tianjin University for the kind invitation.
