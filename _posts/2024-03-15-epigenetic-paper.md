Our new paper has been published in the **Journal of Theoretical Biology**:

> **Huang, R.**, Situ, Q., **Lei, J.\*** (2024). Dynamics of cell-type transition mediated by epigenetic modifications. *Journal of Theoretical Biology*, **577**:111664.

The paper presents a mathematical model of cell-type transitions driven by epigenetic modifications. We investigate how the interplay between DNA methylation, histone modification, and gene regulatory circuits governs the stability and reversibility of cell states. The model predicts the conditions under which cancer cells can undergo epigenetic reprogramming, with implications for understanding tumour heterogeneity and therapeutic resistance.

Codes used in this study are available on GitHub: [jinzhilei/QCIC-Model](https://github.com/jinzhilei/QCIC-Model).
