Prof. Lei's Chinese monograph **《系统生物学——建模、分析、模拟》** has been published by Science Press (科学出版社) in 2024. This is the updated Chinese edition following the 2021 Springer English monograph.

The book provides a comprehensive treatment of systems biology from a mathematical perspective, covering:
- Deterministic and stochastic modelling of biological networks
- Multi-scale modelling of cellular dynamics
- Applications to cancer, haematopoiesis, and developmental biology
- Computational tools and numerical methods

The book is intended for graduate students and researchers in applied mathematics, computational biology, and bioinformatics.
