We are pleased to announce the publication of our new paper in **npj Systems Biology and Applications**:

> Lai, X., Jiao, X., Zhang, H., **Lei, J.\*** (2024). Computational modeling reveals key factors driving treatment-free remission in chronic myeloid leukemic patients. *npj Systems Biology and Applications*, **10**, 45.

This study develops a computational model of CML (chronic myeloid leukaemia) dynamics to identify the key biological factors that determine whether patients can successfully discontinue tyrosine kinase inhibitor (TKI) therapy and maintain treatment-free remission (TFR). Using patient data and mathematical simulation, we reveal how immune surveillance, leukaemic stem cell quiescence, and drug pharmacodynamics jointly shape TFR outcomes.

The work has direct implications for personalising therapy cessation decisions in CML patients.
