Principal Investigator

Jinzhi Lei (雷锦志)
Professor · Doctoral Supervisor · Distinguished Professor of Tianjin (天津市特聘教授)

School of Mathematical Sciences & Center for Applied Mathematics, Tiangong University
Tianjin 300387, P.R. China  |  jzlei@tiangong.edu.cn

Prof. Jinzhi Lei received his B.Sc. (1995), M.Sc. (1998), and Ph.D. (2001) in Applied Mathematics and Analytical Mechanics from Beijing University of Aeronautics and Astronautics (Beihang University). He completed postdoctoral training at Tsinghua University (2001–2003) and subsequently served as Research Assistant and Associate Researcher at the Zhou Pei-Yuan Center for Applied Mathematics, Tsinghua University (2003–2019). In January 2020 he joined Tiangong University as a full Professor and was appointed as Distinguished Professor of Tianjin (天津市特聘教授).

His international experience includes appointments as Visiting Assistant Professor at the University of California, Irvine (2004–2005), Exchange Scholar at McGill University (2005), and multiple visiting positions at UC Irvine, McGill University, Nanyang Technological University, and KTH Royal Institute of Technology, Sweden.

His research bridges mathematics and biology, with core interests in differential equations and dynamical systems, mathematical biology (gene regulatory network modeling, protein folding), and cancer systems biology (cancer evolution dynamics, drug resistance, computational oncology). He has published over 90 peer-reviewed articles (17,000+ citations on Google Scholar), authored two monographs on systems biology (Springer, 2021; Shanghai Scientific Press, 2010), and secured continuous NSFC funding since 2004, including the current key project NSFC 12331018 (2024–2028).

Academic service: Deputy Director, Committee on Mathematical Life Sciences, CSIAM; Executive Committee Member, Committee on Computational Systems Biology, ORS; Editorial Board Member, Mathematical Biosciences and Engineering; Editorial Board Member, Mathematics in Applied Sciences and Engineering.

Join Us

We welcome applications from students and researchers interested in mathematical biology, computational systems biology, and related fields. We particularly encourage applicants with strong backgrounds in:

  • Applied mathematics (differential equations, dynamical systems, probability)
  • Computational biology or bioinformatics
  • Physics or engineering with interest in biological systems

For prospective PhD / Master’s students: Tiangong University admits graduate students annually. Please contact Prof. Lei by email with your CV and a brief description of your research interests.

For postdoctoral researchers: Competitive positions may be available. Please send your CV, a research statement, and two reference letters to jzlei@tiangong.edu.cn.
